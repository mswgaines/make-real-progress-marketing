- Tables have word-wrap issues (e.g., "Mileston e", "Utilizatio n under 30%")
- Typography is basic/utilitarian — needs brand fonts (Playfair Display + DM Sans)
- No brand logo or cover image on pages
- Page numbers are bare with no footer branding
